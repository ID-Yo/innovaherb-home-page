Our Origin

From Bulgarian Forests to Your Daily Ritual

InnoVAherb was founded with a simple yet powerful idea: bring the remarkable health benefits of medicinal mushrooms to everyone through a convenient, fast-absorbing spray format.

Bulgaria has a rich tradition of mushroom foraging and natural medicine stretching back centuries. Our founders grew up surrounded by pristine mountain forests, learning the healing power of fungi from their grandparents.

Today, we combine that ancestral knowledge with cutting-edge dual extraction technology to create the most bioavailable mushroom supplements on the market.
